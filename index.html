About Our System

The Food Bank & Social Voucher System is specially developed to simplify the management of food assistance for students in need. We provide an integrated, transparent digital platform to ensure that every contribution reaches its intended target group.

Our Platform Community

This system connects three main user groups:

💼 HEP Staff

  • Manage the inventory of received food items.
  • Monitor and approve distribution assistance requests.
  • View analytical statistics of distributions and remaining item stock.

View Staff Screen →

🎁 Donors

  • Log new item contributions into the system.
  • Review the history of previous donations.
  • Help monitor the current needs of the food bank.

View Donor Screen →

🎓 Students

  • Check eligibility status and active voucher balance.
  • View the list of currently available food items.
  • Claim food items using digital vouchers.
